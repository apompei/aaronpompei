User Needs

Productivity and well being should not be oppositional ideals as the later can have greater effect to increase the other. By connecting data from personal productivity sources and civic sources we can create more holistic oppotunities for individuals to optimize their daily routine and enhance its expereince by connecting with the individuals' inate desire to reduce stress, increase safety, and for empowerment with a larger civic voice.
